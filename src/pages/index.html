About Me

I'm Derick, a designer-turned-developer who's been working professionally for over 10 years. Based out of Southern California, I enjoy covering topics such as coding, tech, and anything else I find interesting on my YouTube channel. I'm no Mark Zuckerburg, but I can still build some pretty cool stuff.

Before fully transitioning to the world of dev, I was a graphic designer. Design is actually what I majored in in college however, after seeing David Fincher's magnum opus The Social Network in 2010, I was inspired to pursue web development and taught myself how to code. The rest is history.

When I'm not debugging or getting lost in documentation, you can catch me at the gym, a snobby coffee joint, the beach, playing videos games, or messing around with my camera gear. I enjoy all things tech and love picking up new skills. I believe if you're not learning something new, you're not growing.
